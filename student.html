SAMS Welcomes You!!!

SAMS is a platform which makes life in the University of Buea very easy for both the administration (lecturers) and the students. It ensures smooths recording of attendance and follow up and keeps track when a student enters (lectures start) and lives (lectures ends) for each course.


A B O U T U S

SAMS is a secure, efficient and collaborative platform which records and stores the attendances of students in an orderly, appetizing and entricking manner.
Moreover, It safes time, reduce congestion, the long quee and fighting amongs students, and it completely stops fraud in the sense of students writting the names of their fellow students whom are not present for lecture.
Futhermore, It ends the stress and saves the energy of lectures/course delegates moving around with attendance list.
